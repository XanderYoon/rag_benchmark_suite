It explicitly models various facets of the query based on retrieved information and generates a concise response that adheres to length constraints. It generates the response in three steps by: (1) retrieving top relevant passages from the corpus, (2) curating retrieved context for response generation, and (3) synthe- sizing the collected information into a final response; see Figure 1. Our implementation adopts a modular architecture, with clearly separated components for each stage of the pipeline. This design allows for flexible experimentation and independent development of each component.
