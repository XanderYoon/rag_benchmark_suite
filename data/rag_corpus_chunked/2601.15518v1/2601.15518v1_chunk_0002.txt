For dense retrieval, we use the BGE-M3 model [2] to generate query embeddings, and perform dot product similarity search on pre-computed Wikipedia paragraph embeddings from the June 2024 Wikipedia dump [12]. We aggregate paragraph-level scores to article-level scores by taking the maximum paragraph score for each article (i.e max pooling).
