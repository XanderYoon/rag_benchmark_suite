We assess retrieval methods, including BM25, BioBERT, MedCPT, and a hybrid approach, alongside data stores such as Elasticsearch, MongoDB, and FAISS. Despite strong per- formance, some limitations remain. The reliance on OpenAI’s GPT-3.5 for text generation poses reproducibility challenges due to model updates and API latency. Additionally, retriever and database system evaluations remain limited, requiring broader comparisons. Future work should explore additional retrievers and evalu- ate alternative databases for indexing efficiency. Efforts should also focus on retrieval optimization, integrating open-source LLMs, and enabling real-time biomedical applications. Our work highlights trade-offs in retrieval depth, efficiency, and scalability.
