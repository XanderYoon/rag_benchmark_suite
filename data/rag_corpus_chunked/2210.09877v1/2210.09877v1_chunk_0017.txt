Conclusion Through this work, we explore the potential of using Wikipedia concepts to drive the proactive search experience of an IR system working on noisy documents. We explored the usefulness of Wikipedia concepts focusing on 2 research questions. We treated the query description in a podcast segment retrieval task dataset as the user context in a proactive IR system and ran a series of experiments. Our first experiment showed that the overlap of Wikipedia concepts between the relevant segments and the query are significantly larger than with the non-relevant segments.
