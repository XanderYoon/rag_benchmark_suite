The framework is capable of accommodating a variety of downstream appli- cations by enabling the implementation of decoding algorithms that are cus- tomizable and influenced by the probabilities of reflection tokens [196][197]. 9.5.2 Revolutionary Implementations of Self-Evaluation In comparison to traditional RAG methods and state-of-the-art language models, Self,RAG exhibits substantial performance enhancements across a variety of tasks [195][196][197].
