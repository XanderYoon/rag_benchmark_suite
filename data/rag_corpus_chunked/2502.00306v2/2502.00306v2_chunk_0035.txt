Length Dependency. The documents targeted by our attack must be sufficiently long to provide enough information for generating meaningful questions. Applications involving short documents (e.g., 2-3 lines) may lack the necessary content to generate 30 distinct and effective questions. This limitation is less critical in domain-specific RAG applications, where documents are typically longer and rich enough in content to justify the use of a RAG system. Generic Documents.
