RoutIR: Fast Serving of Retrieval Pipelines for RAG 13 7 Summary In this work, we introducedRoutIR, a simple and robust toolkit for wrapping and serving retrieval models to serve online queries. We described the design principles and architecture ofRoutIRand presented several use cases both in configuring the service and integration with RAG pipelines. This highlighted its ability to increase query throughput, a particularly desirable feature for RAG systems.RoutIRhas demonstrated its effectiveness and reliability as the back- bone for the 2025 JHU SCALE Workshop for more than 50 researchers and also as the search service for 2025 TREC RAGTIME Track.
