In this con- text, RAG can be leveraged to augment traditional recommendation algorithms with real-time, diverse information retrieval. By incor- porating RAG, recommendation systems can go beyond relying solely on user preferences and item characteristics stored in a static database. Instead, they can dynamically fetch and consider addi- tional relevant data such as recent trends, user reviews, expert opinions, or even real-time market data [14].
