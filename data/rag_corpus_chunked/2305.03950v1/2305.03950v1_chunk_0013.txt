We also observe that, for some languages, queries generated for other languages can improve the effectiveness of target queries in a different language. For instance, using queries generated for Japanese (Ja) can improve the effectiveness of target queries in Korean (ko), while using Russian (Ru) generated queries can help target queries in Telugu (Te). These results suggest that the embeddings of the queries generated for any single language potentially can also provide useful information for target queries in other languages. 6 CONCLUSION AND FUTURE WORK In this paper we propose a passage embedding augmentation ap- proach to enhance the effectiveness of cross-lingual DRs. Our method can be applied to any cross-lingual DR and it requires no further DR training nor changes to the retrieval pipeline. We empirically showed the method is effective for the cross-lingual DPR method across different backbones. However, a limitation of our empirical investigation is that we did not evaluate the method across other dense retriever architectures.
