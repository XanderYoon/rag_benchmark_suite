Rate and Mistake Reappearance Rate [91] have been developed to measure a model’s ability to avoid generating incorrect or misleading content. Other advanced metrics include Answer Relevance [33], which assesses the precision of responses to queries, Kendall’s tau [117], used for evaluating the accuracy of system rankings, and Micro-F1 [117], which fine-tunes accuracy evaluation in tasks involving multiple correct answers. Prediction Accuracy further complements these by directly measuring how closely the generated responses align with the expected answers, offering a clear measure of a system’s effectiveness in producing accurate content. , Vol. 1, No. 1, Article .
