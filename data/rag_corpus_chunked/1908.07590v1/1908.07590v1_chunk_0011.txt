Finally, we produce 2069 candidate sentences that may connect to real sound effects. Then we label all of the candidate sentences through crowd- sourcing. This time, the human labelers are given sentences with high- lighted candidate triggers, and are asked to determine whether the event-triggering-sounds should be played.
