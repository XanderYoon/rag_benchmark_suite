Docid granularity. We can define text-based docids at different levels of granularity. For example, when using the title, we essen- tially define the docid at the document level, where a single docid represents the entire document. In contrast, for an n-gram-based docid, any substring at any position within the document can serve as a docid to characterize the document, making it a special case of a multi-docid design.
