Benchmarks with temporal queries, named RuleQA-I, RuleQA-Y and RuleQA-W, are constructed based on three temporal KGs, ICEWS14 (García-Durán et al., 2018), YAGO (Mahdisoltani et al., 2013) and WIKI (Leblay and Chekol, 2018). Bench- marks with static queries, named RuleQA-F and RuleQA-N, are constructed based on two static KGs, FB15K-237 (Toutanova and Chen, 2015) and NELL-995 (Xiong et al., 2017).
